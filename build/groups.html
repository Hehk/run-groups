  19. ATX Women's Run Club

    Founded in 2016 when a group of women were looking for buddies to safely run in the dark early mornings. It has now turned it an amazing community of runners of all ages and levels. Each run is focused on building community and honoring where each individual is at on their own personal running journey. Each run has variable length and speed options so everyone is able to run their own pace and distance than meet after for social time.

  22. Jester King Run Club

    Jester King Run Club brings together like-minded running enthusiasts and craft beer fans with a weekly run through our natural landscape in the Texas Hill country. Whether you're a first time or seasoned trail runner, you'll enjoy your time at Jester King. We meet every Thursday at Jester King. If this is your first time with the Jester King Run Club, please check-in at the host stand so you can sign a waiver and get your stamp for one free beer after your run. We meet just outside the Pole Barn/Pasture Bar between 6-6:30pm. OUR 2-MILE TRAIL RUN STARTS AT 6:30PM. See you there!

    Website Facebook
